About this ranking

Each qualifying player is simulated in Bo3 against every other qualifier on the chosen surface, using Barnett–Clarke point-on-serve probabilities and exact Markov-chain recursion over games, tiebreaks and sets. Field W% is the average of those analytic win probabilities — a reading of 80% means the player beats a random qualifying peer four out of five times.

Qualification: at least 20 tour-level matches on the selected surface over the rolling last 52 weeks (not the calendar year). Adjust with the Min matches slider below, or use All Surfaces to rank across a player's full 52-week sample.
