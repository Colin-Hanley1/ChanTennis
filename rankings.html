About this ranking

Each qualifying player is simulated in Bo3 against every other qualifier on the chosen surface, using Barnett–Clarke point-on-serve probabilities and exact Markov-chain recursion. Each pair's win probability is blended with a standard Elo expected score, weighted by the Elo blend slider. Field W% is the average across the peer pool.

Qualification: at least 20 tour-level matches on the selected surface over the rolling last 52 weeks (not the calendar year). Adjust with the Min matches slider below, or use All Surfaces to rank across a player's full 52-week sample.
